Welcome to East West University

East West University is a private university located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was founded in 1996 by a group of academics and business leaders with the aim of providing world-class education to students in Bangladesh.

At East West University, we offer a range of undergraduate and graduate programs across a variety of fields, including business, engineering, and social sciences. Our faculty is made up of highly qualified academics and industry professionals who are committed to providing our students with a challenging and rewarding education.
